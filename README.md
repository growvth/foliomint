## Why FolioMint

A personal site should not be a side quest. FolioMint is for people who want a clear, modern presence online without fighting templates or infrastructure. You own the story. We help with structure, layout, and getting you live.

## What you can do

- **Resume to site in one flow** Start from your file and shape it in a guided path.
- **Edit with a live preview** Tune profile, experience, projects, and more with feedback as you go.
- **Pick a look that fits** Themes built for readability and a bit of personality.
- **Add a blog** Write in your own voice next to your portfolio.
- **Link out** GitHub, LinkedIn, and the rest, so people can dig deeper.
- **Scale up when it makes sense** More room and premium options when you need them.

## Who it is for

Students, people changing paths, and builders who want one link that feels like them, for jobs, clients, or your network, without maintaining a custom stack.
